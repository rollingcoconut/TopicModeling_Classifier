Rumor has it, the ‘Maleficent’ actress, Angelina Jolie is picking out her burial gown.

Speaking with National Enquirer, a source said; “Angelina’s already set aside the dress she wants to wear for eternity.”

The source added that the actress wants to wear “a beautiful, black, $5,000 dress” designed by her late friend L’Wren Scott.

“She wants to be buried in it in honor of her only female friend. She knows L’Wren would have loved the idea, Angie’s trying not to be sentimental about it,” said the source.

Angelina hasn’t utter a word on the reports as at the time of this publication. However, report on Gossip Cop quoting its own sources claim that the rumours about Angelina’s funeral are “not true.”
